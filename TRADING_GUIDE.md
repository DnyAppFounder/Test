# Trading Guide - How to Use Real Trading Features

## Quick Start

### 1. Setup Your Wallet

**First Time Users:**
1. Open the app
2. Choose "Create New Wallet" or "Import Wallet"
3. **SAVE YOUR RECOVERY PHRASE** - Write it down securely
4. Fund your wallet with SOL (needed for transactions and fees)

**Important**: You need SOL for:
- Gas fees (transaction costs)
- Trading (buying tokens with SOL)
- Swapping tokens

---

## 2. Discover Tokens

### Market Discovery Tab
1. Go to the **"Discover"** tab (default view)
2. Browse real Solana tokens with live data
3. Use filters to find tokens:
   - **Trending**: Popular tokens right now
   - **New**: Recently listed tokens
   - **Boosted**: Promoted tokens
   - **Volume**: High trading volume tokens
   - **Pump.fun**: New pump.fun tokens

### Search
- Tap the search bar
- Enter token name, symbol, or contract address
- See real-time results from DexScreener and Jupiter

---

## 3. View Token Details

**From any token card:**
1. Tap on a token
2. You'll see:
   - **Live Price Chart** with timeframes (1H, 4H, 1D, 1W, 1M)
   - **Current Price** and 24h change
   - **Trading Interface** (Buy/Sell/Swap)
   - **Stats**: Volume, Liquidity, Market Cap
   - **Contract Address** (tap to copy)

---

## 4. Execute Real Trades

### Option A: From Token Detail Page

**Buy Token:**
1. Open any token detail page
2. Select **"Buy"** mode
3. Enter amount in SOL
4. Review quote:
   - Amount you'll receive
   - Minimum received (with slippage)
   - Price impact
5. Tap **"Confirm Buy"**
6. Wait for signing → sending → confirmation
7. Transaction signature will appear
8. Your tokens appear in "My Assets"

**Sell Token:**
1. Open a token you own
2. Select **"Sell"** mode
3. Enter amount to sell
4. Review quote showing SOL you'll receive
5. Tap **"Confirm Sell"**
6. Wait for confirmation
7. SOL appears in your wallet

### Option B: Dedicated Swap Screen

**Swap Any Token:**
1. Go to **Swap** from bottom navigation or "My Assets" actions
2. Select "From" token
3. Select "To" token
4. Enter amount
5. Auto-fetches real quote from Jupiter
6. Shows:
   - Estimated output
   - Price impact
   - Route information
7. Tap **"Swap"**
8. Transaction executes on-chain
9. Balances update automatically

---

## 5. Understanding Trading Info

### Quote Details

**Amount You'll Receive**: Expected output amount
**Minimum Received**: Worst case with slippage protection
**Price Impact**: How much your trade affects the price
- Green (< 1%): Low impact
- Yellow (1-5%): Moderate impact
- Red (> 5%): High impact - be careful!

**Slippage Tolerance**: Default is 0.5%
- Price can change this much before trade fails
- Protects against unfavorable price movements

### Transaction Status

**Fetching quote**: Getting latest prices from Jupiter
**Quote ready**: Ready to execute
**Signing**: Creating transaction signature with your wallet
**Sending**: Broadcasting to Solana network
**Confirming**: Waiting for blockchain confirmation
**Success**: Trade completed! Shows transaction signature
**Error**: Trade failed - see error message

## 7. Safety Tips

### Before Trading
✅ Start with small amounts to test
✅ Check price impact (avoid > 5%)
✅ Verify token contract address
✅ Ensure you have SOL for gas fees
✅ Double-check amounts before confirming

### Security
✅ **NEVER share your recovery phrase**
✅ Keep recovery phrase offline and secure
✅ Don't screenshot your recovery phrase
✅ Only trade tokens you've researched
✅ Be wary of tokens with low liquidity

### Gas Fees
- Every transaction costs a small amount of SOL (usually ~0.000005 SOL)
- Keep at least 0.01 SOL in wallet for fees
- Failed transactions still consume fees

---

## 8. Troubleshooting

### "Failed to get quote"
- Check internet connection
- Token might have very low liquidity
- Try reducing trade amount

### "Transaction failed"
- Insufficient SOL for gas fees
- Price moved beyond slippage tolerance
- Try again with higher slippage (be careful!)

### "Wallet not available"
- Restart the app
- Re-import wallet if needed
- Check that wallet has been created/imported

### Tokens not showing after trade
- Wait a few seconds for confirmation
- Pull down to manually refresh
- Check transaction on Solscan to confirm success

## Example Trade Flow

**Buying a Token (Start to Finish)**

1. **Discover**
   - Browse "Trending" category
   - Find a token (e.g., BONK)
   - Tap to open details

2. **Research**
   - Check 24h chart
   - View liquidity ($500K+)
   - Check volume (high = good)
   - Note price impact

3. **Prepare**
   - Ensure you have SOL (0.1+ SOL)
   - Decide amount (e.g., 0.05 SOL)

4. **Execute**
   - Select "Buy" mode
   - Enter "0.05" SOL
   - Review: You'll get ~X BONK
   - Price impact: 0.3% (green)
   - Tap "Confirm Buy"

5. **Wait**
   - Signing... (2 sec)
   - Sending... (3 sec)
   - Confirming... (5-10 sec)
   - Success! 🎉

6. **Verify**
   - See transaction signature
   - Check "My Assets" - BONK appears
   - Portfolio value updated
   - Verified on Solscan

**Total time**: ~15-20 seconds
